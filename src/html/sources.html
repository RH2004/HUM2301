Sources & Bibliography

Below are the key books, hadith references, papers, and curated videos used in our research and presentation.

Key books

  • Safiur Rahman al-Mubarakpuri, The Sealed Nectar (Ar-Raheeq Al-Makhtum).
  • Ibn Ishaq (translated by Alfred Guillaume), The Life of Muhammad.
  • Tariq Ramadan, In the Footsteps of the Prophet.

Hadith & classical texts

  • Sahih al-Bukhari — references to Khadija and early seerah.
  • Sahih Muslim — relevant narrations about the Prophet's grief and remembrance.

Academic & modern resources

  • Yaqeen Institute — papers on faith and coping with grief.
  • Islamic studies journals — seerah articles on the Year of Sorrow.

Curated videos (YouTube)

  • The Death of Khadijah — Ustadh Abdulrahman
  • Prophet Muhammad’s Year of Sorrow: Lessons For Today
  • The UNTOLD Story of Khadija (RA)
