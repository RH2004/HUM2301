The 3D Model — Design & Materials

Our physical model is divided into three sections: the House, the Road, and the Cemetery. It uses light to represent the Prophet's inner light and the moment of grief when it fades.

Materials

  • Cardboard / foamboard for structure
  • Sandpaper and real sand for the road
  • Clay & stones for the cemetery
  • Battery-powered LED bulb, wiring, and a small switch
  • Paint (matte black, beige, gold accents), labels

Construction notes

  1. Base: a rectangular platform (40x20 cm). Mark three zones: house, road, cemetery.
  2. House: small cardboard enclosure; fix LED inside and wire to a hidden switch.
  3. Road: sand texture fixed with diluted PVA glue; sprinkle small pebbles and footprints near the edge.
  4. Cemetery: low stone graves with one symbolic marker for Khadija (no inscription).
  5. Finishing: gold leaf accents at the house entrance to represent warmth; soft matte paint elsewhere.
