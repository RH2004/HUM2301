“The Year of Sorrow”

A tribute to Khadija bint Khuwaylid (RA) — love, loss, and the Prophet’s humanity.

This project accompanies a physical 3D model that traces a journey from home to the cemetery, using light and materiality to symbolise memory, grief, and devotion.
