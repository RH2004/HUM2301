This model represents the journey of loss and remembrance, following the death of Khadija, may God be pleased with her. It is divided into three sections. First, the House, with its warm light, symbolizes the love and security Khadija provided. Second, the Road, a textured path, represents the difficult journey of grief and transition. Finally, the Cemetery, a quiet and simple space, signifies memory and the enduring legacy of a life well-lived. This model invites reflection on love, loss, and the human heart.
